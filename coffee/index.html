Our Menu

  • Hot Coffee

    A freshly brewed cup of rich, aromatic coffee made from premium coffee beans. Smooth, warm, and comforting,

  • Espresso

    A strong, concentrated coffee made by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee beans.

  • Mocha

    A delicious blend of espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup, often topped with whipped cream.

  • Cold Brew

    Coffee grounds steeped in cold water for 12–24 hours, producing a smooth, naturally sweet, and less acidic coffee.

  • Macchiato

    An espresso "marked" with a small amount of steamed milk or milk foam, balancing the bold espresso taste.

  • Starbucks mocha latte

    premium chocolate, creating a perfectly balanced coffee with a creamy texture and indulgent chocolate flavor
